About SOWCoders

The Society Of Women Coders (SOWCoders) is a volunteer-run 501 (c)3 non-profit organization led by women working in Technology. Our mission is to encourage young girls from developing nations to opt for careers in STEM ( Science,Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) by conducting free coding, entrepreneurship & information camps for them in their home ground as well as virtually.

Their mission is to help young girls and women:

  • Gain access to higher paying jobs, especially in careers in technology, and to;
  • Equip themselves to thrive in our technologically advancing world.

They accomplish these goals by:

  • Conducting coding camps where women get get-on experience in coding and STEM-related projects;
  • Teaching basic computer literacy skills to ensure the disenfranchised have opportunities to participate, and by;
  • Providing mentorship to alumni from experienced mentors.
